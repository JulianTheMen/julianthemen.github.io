Our ideologies

Our ideologies are mostly central and progressive.
We want to be progressive because in our ideal plan we are going to change the future. We will be doing this by using new technologies and also by investing in the environment.
We are central because we have both right and left winged opinions. Take housing problems for example, we have a rather right winged opinion about that; We want to build more and cheaper houses and we would like to invest in more in companies. But on the other side...
